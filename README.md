To create a tables in markdown you need (Example) :
````markdown
*Sample* | *Text*
------ | ----
1234 | 5
````

_Sample_ | _Text_
------ | ----
1234 | 5

For center:
````markdown
_Sample_ | _Text_
:------: | :----:
1234 | 5
````

_Sample_ | _Text_
:------: | :----:
1234 | 5

For right:
````markdown
_Sample_ | _Text_
-----: | ----:
1234 | 5
````

_Sample_ | _Text_
------: | ----:
1234 | 5
